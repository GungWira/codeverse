What Will You Get

Basic Understanding of the MERN Stack

Learn how MERN technologies (MongoDB, Express, React, and Node.js) work together, from frontend to backend, with clear and easy-to-understand explanations.

Server and API Development

Master how to build servers and RESTful APIs using Node.js and Express to manage data and communication between applications.

User Interface Development with React

Learn how to use React to build interactive, dynamic, and responsive user interfaces.

MongoDB Database Management

Learn how to manage and optimize NoSQL MongoDB databases to store and access application data quickly and efficiently.

About Narator

Mr. Toni Santoso

Backend Lecture

I am Ananta Darmawangsa, a frontend developer with a passion for teaching. Currently, I play a role as a lecturer at Codeverse, a leading programming training institution. For the past 5 years, I have helped thousands of students learn coding and change their lives by becoming a developer. I have also been invited by companies such as Google and Facebook to teach their employees.

My journey in the world of programming began when I was 12 years old, aspiring to create my own Space Invader game. Since then, I have created hundreds of websites, applications, and games. However, most importantly, I realized that my biggest passion is teaching.

Most of my time is spent researching how to make learning coding fun and how difficult concepts can be easily understood. My discoveries are then applied in my bootcamp courses. In my courses, you will find a lot of geek humor, but also many explanations and animations to make sure everything is easy to understand.

I will always be there to help you at every step of your learning journey.
